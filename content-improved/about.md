## Our Standards

CNP News follows rigorous editorial standards to ensure accuracy, transparency, and independence.

### Independence

We maintain strict editorial independence:
- **No pay-for-play:** We do not accept payment for coverage
- **No vendor influence:** Advertisers have no input into editorial decisions
- **Financial disclosure:** Staff cannot own securities in companies they cover
- **Review samples:** We disclose when products are provided for review (and return them after testing)

### Fact-Checking

Every article undergoes verification before publication:
1. **Primary sources:** We link to original documents (official announcements, regulatory filings, research papers)
2. **Two-source rule:** Factual claims require at least two independent sources
3. **Expert verification:** Technical claims are reviewed by domain experts
4. **Pre-publication review:** All articles reviewed by senior editors

### Corrections

We correct errors promptly and transparently:
- **Speed:** Corrections issued within 24 hours of discovery
- **Visibility:** Correction notices appear at top of articles
- **Public log:** All corrections recorded in public [Corrections Log](/corrections-log)
- **Accountability:** Email corrections@cnpnews.net to report errors

See full [Corrections Policy](/corrections).

### AI Disclosure

We use AI tools (Claude 3.5, GPT-4) for research assistance and draft generation, but:
- **Human editors verify all facts** against primary sources
- **Human editors make all editorial decisions** on framing and publication
- **Every AI-assisted article** displays a disclosure notice
- **AI limitations are acknowledged** (hallucinations, bias, staleness)

See full [AI Disclosure Policy](/ai-disclosure).

### Affiliate Disclosure

Reviews and comparisons may contain affiliate links. We:
- **Test everything ourselves:** We purchase or trial all products independently
- **Write honestly:** Affiliate relationships do not influence our verdicts
- **Disclose clearly:** All reviews display affiliate disclosure notices

See full [Ethics & Disclosures](/ethics-disclosures).

## Our Coverage

CNP News publishes four types of content:

### 1. **Explainers** — Deep dives into technical topics
*Example: [RAG Architecture Patterns: Latency, Cost, Quality Trade-offs](/rag-architecture-patterns)*

Our explainers break down complex infrastructure decisions with hands-on analysis, performance benchmarks, and decision frameworks.

### 2. **Comparisons & Reviews** — Hands-on product evaluations
*Example: [Weaviate vs Pinecone vs Milvus: Vector Database Comparison](/weaviate-pinecone-milvus-comparison)*

We test products in production-like scenarios and provide decision matrices for specific use cases.

### 3. **News & Analysis** — Breaking developments and policy shifts
*Example: [OpenAI Unveils Enterprise Controls for LLM Governance](/openai-enterprise-controls)*

Time-sensitive coverage of product launches, regulatory changes, and market shifts that affect deployment decisions.

### 4. **Trackers** — Ongoing monitoring of key metrics
*Example: [GPU Lead Times & Pricing Tracker](/gpu-lead-times-pricing-tracker)*

Monthly or quarterly updates on supply chain dynamics, benchmark scores, and regulatory developments.

## Our Editorial Process

### Story Development
1. **Brief creation:** Editors identify gaps in coverage based on operator feedback and market developments
2. **Research:** Editors gather primary sources and conduct interviews
3. **Drafting:** Writers create articles following our [Editorial Policy](/editorial-policy)
4. **Fact-checking:** All factual claims verified against primary sources
5. **Senior review:** Senior editors approve all content before publication
6. **Publication:** Articles published with proper disclosures and citations
7. **Updates:** We update evergreen content quarterly or when significant developments occur

### AI-Assisted Workflow (Optional)
For some articles, we use AI to accelerate research and drafting:
1. **Human brief:** Editor creates detailed brief with sources and key points
2. **AI draft:** AI generates initial draft based on brief
3. **Human rewrite:** Editor substantially rewrites to add expertise and verify facts
4. **Fact-check:** All AI-generated claims verified against primary sources
5. **Senior review:** Same approval process as non-AI content
6. **Disclosure:** AI assistance disclosed at top of article
