# Publishing Checklist

Before any article goes live, complete the following steps to ensure quality, compliance, and performance.

## Editorial Review
1. **E‑E‑A‑T Verification:** Confirm the author is a qualified expert and that the content demonstrates experience, expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness【490377143752729†L10-L16】.
2. **Value‑Add Check:** Ensure aggregated stories include unique context, analysis, or research【490377143752729†L62-L74】. Use the Value‑Add Checklist.
3. **Accuracy & Sources:** Verify all facts, figures, and quotations. Include citations or external links as appropriate.
4. **Byline & Bio:** Confirm the byline is correct and the author bio is present and linked.
5. **Disclosures:** Add sponsorship, affiliate, or AI assistance disclosures where necessary.

## Technical Review
1. **Category & Tags:** Assign exactly one category and appropriate tags from the approved allowlist【490377143752729†L205-L213】.
2. **Slug & Permalink:** Ensure the URL is concise and reflects the primary keyword or topic.
3. **Canonical Tag:** Verify the canonical URL is absolute and self‑referencing (for paginated content)【490377143752729†L194-L203】.
4. **Schema Markup:** Embed valid structured data (NewsArticle, Person, Review, etc.) and test it.
5. **Images:** Compress and resize images; use proper alt text; preload the LCP image【490377143752729†L302-L317】.
6. **Internal Links:** Add links to the Pillar Hub, related Cluster Pages, and relevant evergreen articles【490377143752729†L383-L391】.
7. **External Links:** Use `nofollow` for non‑editorial or user‑generated links. Affiliate links must use `rel="sponsored"`【490377143752729†L40-L56】.
8. **Performance:** Run a quick Lighthouse test or use a browser extension to check LCP and INP. Defer or remove heavy scripts if needed【490377143752729†L248-L259】.

## Final Steps
1. **Preview:** View the article on mobile and desktop to confirm formatting, readability, and responsiveness.
2. **Publication:** Schedule or publish the article. Verify that it appears in the correct category archive and feeds.
3. **Ping & Sitemap:** If applicable, ping search engines and ensure sitemaps reflect the new post.
4. **Promotion:** Share on social channels or syndication partners as appropriate. Add to Flipboard magazines and newsletters.
5. **Monitoring:** Watch GA4 real‑time and GSC for indexing issues. Respond to comments and questions from readers.
