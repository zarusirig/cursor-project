# Director of Digital Strategy & Authority

## Role Overview
The Director of Digital Strategy & Authority is the overall steward of our SEO blueprint. This person ensures that CNP News operates within Google’s evolving policies and consistently demonstrates high E‑E‑A‑T standards.

- **Cross‑Team Coordination:** Bridge executive, editorial, technical, and growth teams. Resolve conflicts (e.g., performance vs visual design) and prioritise work.
- **E‑E‑A‑T Oversight:** Institutionalise the E‑E‑A‑T framework by ensuring trustworthy authorship, rigorous fact‑checking, and clear disclosures【490377143752729†L10-L17】.

## Required Skills
- Deep knowledge of SEO, content strategy, and WordPress architecture.
- Familiarity with Google’s policies, including SRA and scaled content guidelines.
- Strong leadership and communication skills to align cross‑functional teams.

## Success Metrics
- Zero major policy violations; clean GSC coverage and structured data reports.
- Increase in organic traffic and Discover inclusion; stable or improved Core Web Vitals.
- Effective collaboration across teams as reflected in project completion and feedback.
