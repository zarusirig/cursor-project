# Audience Development / Growth Manager

## Role Overview
The Growth Manager drives audience acquisition, retention, and monetisation. By leveraging data and experimentation, they expand our reach and increase reader loyalty without compromising editorial integrity.

## Responsibilities
- **CTR Optimisation:** Identify low‑CTR pages and run structured A/B tests on headlines and meta descriptions【490377143752729†L457-L467】. Use the Headline CTR Playbook to plan and document tests.
- **Syndication Management:** Manage relationships with syndication partners (e.g., Medium, Flipboard). Ensure partners use `rel="canonical"` to point back to the original article and enforce 301 redirects on expired content【490377143752729†L474-L485】.
- **CTA Strategy:** Optimise calls to action for newsletter signups and product recommendations. Avoid generic CTAs; design context‑specific prompts【490377143752729†L495-L499】.
- **Distribution Channels:** Curate Flipboard magazines and other niche channels. Tailor headlines and summaries for each platform while maintaining topical accuracy.
- **Audience Segmentation:** Use GA4 and RCI to identify Casual vs Loyal readers and create targeted campaigns.
- **Affiliate Growth:** Collaborate with the Content Strategist to identify high‑value affiliate opportunities. Monitor conversion rates and adjust promotional strategies.

## Required Skills
- Experience with digital marketing, SEO, and audience development.
- Strong analytical skills and familiarity with A/B testing methodologies.
- Relationship management skills for partnering with external platforms.

## Success Metrics
- CTR improvements on tested pages; increase in average pages per session.
- Growth in newsletter signups and affiliate conversions.
- Successful syndication partnerships with proper canonical implementation and no loss of original ranking.
