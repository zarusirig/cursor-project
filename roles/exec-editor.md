# Executive Editor (Content Quality & E‑E‑A‑T)

## Role Overview
The Executive Editor ensures that every piece of content published by CNP News meets the highest standards of accuracy, trust, and value. This role focuses on editorial quality rather than traffic alone.

## Responsibilities
- **E‑E‑A‑T Enforcement:** Verify that articles demonstrate experience, expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness【490377143752729†L10-L17】.
- **Value‑Add Guardian:** Ensure all aggregated or syndicated stories include unique context, analysis, or verification to avoid scaled content abuse【490377143752729†L62-L74】.
- **Corrections & Fact‑Checking:** Oversee the corrections process and ensure transparency in updating articles. Maintain the correction log and update `dateModified` only on meaningful changes【490377143752729†L137-L141】.
- **Bylines & Bios:** Confirm that author bylines and bios are present and complete. Verify authorship credentials and external links.
- **Editorial Policies:** Maintain and update editorial, sourcing, and disclosure policies. Train new writers and editors on these guidelines.

## Required Skills
- Expertise in journalism or technical/business reporting.
- Strong editing and fact‑checking abilities.
- Understanding of SEO best practices and user experience.

## Success Metrics
- >95 % of articles published without factual corrections post‑publication.
- All aggregated articles include value‑add elements.
- Positive reader feedback on clarity and trustworthiness.
