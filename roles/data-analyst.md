# Data Analyst / BI Specialist (The Scorekeeper)

## Role Overview
The Data Analyst builds and maintains our analytics infrastructure. They convert raw data from GA4, GSC, RCI, and other sources into actionable insights that guide editorial and business decisions.

## Responsibilities
- **GA4 Setup:** Configure GA4 with custom events for scroll depth, engaged time, recirculation clicks, and newsletter CTAs (see `/docs/analytics/ga4-setup.md`).
- **GSC Integration:** Monitor GSC performance, coverage, and Core Web Vitals reports. Identify pages with high impressions but low CTR【490377143752729†L457-L462】.
- **Dashboard Creation:** Build Looker Studio dashboards that blend GA4, GSC, and RCI data. Provide weekly reports on key metrics such as traffic growth, engagement, conversion rates, and technical health.
- **RCI (Real‑Time Content Insights):** Analyse CER (Content Engagement Rank) and segment readers into Casual vs Loyal categories to inform content promotion strategies.
- **Data Quality:** Ensure tracking is accurate across site templates. Work with developers to implement event tracking.
- **Insights & Recommendations:** Present findings to the editorial and growth teams. Suggest tests for improving CTR and recirculation.

## Required Skills
- Proficiency in Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, and data visualisation tools (Looker Studio).
- Knowledge of SQL and data processing scripts.
- Ability to translate data into clear, actionable recommendations.

## Success Metrics
- Reliable dashboards with minimal data discrepancies.
- Insight reports delivered on schedule; recommendations lead to measurable improvements in CTR, engagement, and conversions.
- Rapid identification and resolution of tracking issues.
